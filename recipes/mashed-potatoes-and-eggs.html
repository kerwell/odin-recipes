Mashed Potatoes and Eggs: The Ultimate Comfort Food

Home

This dish is known to help get you out of a rut and feel a taste of home with every bite! (Popularized by Chef Amber)

Like the title says, this dish is known for making someone down, feel more warmth. This comfort food adds the fluffiness of mashed potatoes and the heartiness of eggs to fill you up.

Here are the ingredients needed

  • 1 pouch (4.7 oz) Betty Crocker™ Mashed Potato Mix Loaded Mashed
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 4-6 eggs
  • Salt and pepper, for added taste
  • 4-6 slices of fried bacon
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • Pinch of crushed red pepper

Finally, the steps needed!

  1. Preheat oven to 350° F. Heat a large oven-safe skillet over medium heat. Add water and butter and prepare mashed potatoes as directed on package.
  2. Once mashed potatoes are ready, cover with 2/3 of the shredded cheese. Next, make 4-6 wells in the mashed potatoes for the eggs. Crack eggs into the wells and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Cover with remaining cheese (optional).
  3. Place entire skillet in oven and bake until whites of eggs are just set but yolks are still slightly runny, about 12 to 15 minutes. Top with bacon and sprinkle with green onions. Serve!
