Listen when I tell you: This dish mfing slaps

This dish is not ONLY easy to make, but it a nice source of protein. Great for students or those in a budget. Or honestly, just anyone who doesn't want to use the stove because you have to get out a pan and no one wants to clean pans.

Here are the ingredients needed

  • Mug
  • 1-2 eggs
  • Microwave
  • Any additional personal ingredients

Finally, the steps needed!

  1. Crack eggs and place it into mug
  2. Whisk it with a fork till you're left with a yellow aftermath
  3. Place in microwave for a minute, take out and whisk again, place for another minute
